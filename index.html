Main

Mauricio Uriona Maldonado

I study clean technologies driving the transition toward sustainable and low-carbon economies in Developing Countries from a socio-technical perspective. I am an expert in system dynamics modelling which I use to develop simulated scenarios of technology diffusion and sustainable transitions. I lead the Sustainable Energy Innovation Group (Sinergia) at UFSC. My research has been published in several journals and in specialized conferences across the world. I held visiting positions at Universidad Autonoma de Madrid (Spain), University of Tampere (Finland) and University of Bremen (Germany) and received training at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (USA).
